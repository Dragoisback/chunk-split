Welcome to Paragraph Formatter Pro!

This tool helps you quickly format your text for web pages, documents, and more. Here's a quick guide:

Input & Formatting

  • Live Preview: Type or paste your text in the left "Input" panel and see the formatted result on the right.
  • Paragraphs: Create paragraphs by leaving a blank line between blocks of text.
  • Rich Text: Use simple markers for formatting:
    • **bold text** or __bold text__ for bold.
    • *italic text* or _italic text_ for italic.
    • [link text](https://example.com) for links.

Key Features

  • Wrap in

    tags: Check this to wrap each paragraph in proper HTML

    tags, the standard for web content.

  • Clean extra spaces: Automatically collapses multiple spaces or line breaks within a paragraph into a single space.
  • Undo/Redo: Don't worry about mistakes! Use the Undo and Redo buttons to step through your changes.
  • Find & Replace: Use the advanced Find/Replace panel to make bulk edits. It supports regular expressions (Regex) for powerful searching.
  • Project Management: Save your work! Use the "Projects" section to save, load, and manage different drafts. You can even import/export all your projects as a single file.
